ShoppingList - FamilySync

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 15, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At NeoLabsLtd, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how ShoppingList - FamilySync ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our shopping list application.

ShoppingList - FamilySync is designed with privacy by design - we collect only the minimum data necessary to provide you with an excellent shopping experience.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Shopping List Data: Items you add, quantities, categories, and notes
  • Voice Input: Voice recordings when you use voice-to-text features (processed locally on your device)
  • App Preferences: Theme selections, settings, and customizations

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Usage Analytics: App feature usage, screen views, and user interactions (via Firebase Analytics)
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, app version
  • Technical Data: Crash reports, performance metrics, and error logs

Family Sync Information:

  • Anonymous Device ID: Unique identifier for device synchronization
  • Family Code: Temporary sharing codes for list collaboration
  • Shared List Data: Shopping items and changes shared with family members

Advertising Information:

  • Advertising ID: Anonymous identifier for ad personalization
  • Ad Interaction Data: Clicks, views, and engagement metrics
  • General Demographics: Age range and general location for relevant ads

How We Use Your Information

  • Core Functionality: Manage your shopping lists, sync across devices, and provide app features
  • Smart Suggestions: Learn from your shopping patterns to suggest frequently used items
  • App Improvement: Analyze usage patterns to improve features and fix bugs
  • Advertising: Display relevant advertisements and measure ad performance
  • Customer Support: Respond to your questions and provide technical assistance

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We DO NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

Service Providers:

  • Firebase (Google): Analytics, database, and authentication services
  • AdMob (Google): Advertisement serving and analytics
  • Ad Networks: Third-party advertising partners for ad delivery

Legal Requirements:

  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • In connection with a business merger or acquisition

Family Sharing:

  • Shopping list data is shared only with family members you explicitly invite
  • Family sharing uses anonymous codes - no personal information is exchanged

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Firebase Security: Google's enterprise-grade security infrastructure
  • Local Storage: Most data is stored locally on your device
  • Anonymous Authentication: No email or password required for family sharing
  • Limited Access: Only authorized personnel can access aggregated, anonymized data

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in servers located outside your country of residence, including the United States, where Firebase services are hosted. We ensure that all international transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and provide adequate safeguards for your personal information.

Data Retention

  • Shopping List Data: Stored until you delete the app or manually clear data
  • Analytics Data: Retained for up to 26 months (Firebase Analytics default)
  • Voice Recordings: Processed locally and not stored on our servers
  • Family Sync Data: Deleted when you leave family sharing or delete your account
  • Advertising Data: Retained according to advertising partner policies (typically 12-24 months)

Advertising

ShoppingList - FamilySync is supported by advertising revenue to keep the app free for all users:

  • Ad Types: Banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded video ads
  • Ad Personalization: Ads may be personalized based on your interests and usage patterns
  • Third-Party Ads: We work with advertising partners who may collect data for ad targeting
  • Opt-Out: You can limit ad tracking through your device settings
  • Ad-Free Experience: Currently not available - app is fully supported by ads

Children's Privacy

ShoppingList - FamilySync is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

Note: Our advertising partners also comply with COPPA regulations and do not serve targeted ads to children under 13.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Access and Control:

  • View Data: All your shopping list data is visible within the app
  • Edit Information: Modify or delete shopping items at any time
  • Export Data: Share lists or create backups through the app's sharing features

Privacy Settings:

  • Analytics Opt-out: Disable analytics in app settings
  • Voice Input: Choose whether to use voice features
  • Family Sharing: Join or leave family sync at any time

Data Deletion:

  • Clear App Data: Delete all local data through device settings
  • Uninstall App: Removes all local data from your device
  • Account Deletion: Contact us to delete cloud-synced data

Data Deletion Requests

You have the right to request deletion of your personal data from our systems. To submit a data deletion request:

Email Request

Send us a data deletion request via email:

Email: neolabsltd@gmail.com

What Gets Deleted:

  • Firebase Database: All your shopping list data, preferences, and family sync information
  • Analytics Data: Your usage patterns and interaction data
  • Device Identifiers: Anonymous IDs used for synchronization
  • Advertising Data: Any personalization data used for ads

Deletion Timeline:

  • Response Time: We will acknowledge your request within 48 hours
  • Deletion Completion: Data will be deleted within 30 days of your request
  • Confirmation: You will receive confirmation once deletion is complete

Note: After deletion, you will lose access to all your shopping lists, family sync features, and app preferences. This action cannot be undone.
